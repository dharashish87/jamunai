Master Data Management (MDM)

Unify product, customer, and channel data — end “multiple versions of truth” and trust your reports.

Why it matters in India

  • SMBs often have data split across POS, marketplaces, website, and CRM — MDM unifies it.
  • Clean master data improves marketing targeting and reduces returns/support issues.
  • Clear KPI definitions keep finance, sales, and marketing aligned.

What you get

  • Current-state audit of systems & data quality
  • Golden record design (products/customers)
  • Data quality rules (dedupe, standardization)
  • KPI catalog with definitions & owners
  • Integration plan for ongoing syncs

FAQs

Is MDM only for large companies?

No — a lightweight MDM layer saves SMBs time and money by eliminating data chaos.

Typical timelines?

Pilots are usually 4–8 weeks depending on scope and number of systems.
